California Club Chicken Wraps

Description

"I work in a corporate office, and our cafe has a wrap station every Friday. I got hooked on the California club chicken wraps so I watched closely and finally made my own version at home. They are so delicious!" -BethAnne923

Ingredients

Chipotle Mayonnaise:

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 2 chipotle chiles in adobo sauce, finely chopped

Wraps:

  • 2 large spinach tortillas
  • ½ cup shredded lettuce, or to taste
  • 1 ½ cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 Haas avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 4 slices cooked bacon, chopped
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 cooked chicken breasts, cut into chunks
